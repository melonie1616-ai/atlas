# ATLAS — Release Notes

**Release date:** March 17, 2026

---

## What’s new

- **Manage groups:** Shows all existing groups (defaults, saved list, and groups in use). List stays in sync on load and after add/rename/delete; renaming people-only groups now saves correctly.
- **Quick Capture (pink):** Paste or speak notes into the panel; voice dictation in Chrome/Edge. Notes go to Inbox for triage.
- **Report Out:** Executive-style table (Workstream, Scope, Progress, Owner, Milestones, Open items, Key timing). Grouped by Business Unit / Crosscutting; color-coded progress. Copy, Print, AI Summarize.
- **Workstreams:** Status **Not Started** added; dashboard tiles show correct status. **Stakeholders** per workstream (multi-select from People); shown on dashboard tiles and workstream list; editable in Edit Workstream modal.
- **AI Program Insights:** Real counts from the app; Next Steps use actual task owners; shorter bullets with dates and numbers.
- **Program Timeline:** Configurable starting date (date picker next to “Starting”).
- **Dashboard:** Open Items tile shows count due this week (Mon–Sun).

---

## Data added or changed

**Workstreams**
- **Default workstreams (8):** GTM, Legal, HR, Operations (BU); Governance / Policy, Copilot (Enterprise), Data (Enterprise), Nexus / Platform Architecture (Cross-cutting). Each has name, color, category, owner, description, status, notes, phase.
- **Status values:** Not Started, On Track, At Risk, Blocked, Complete.
- **Stakeholders:** Each workstream has an optional list of stakeholders (people by name); displayed on dashboard tiles and Workstreams management table; add/edit in the Edit Workstream modal.

**People & groups**
- **Default groups (8):** CDO Leadership, CDO Team, Executive, Legal, HR, IT/Security, Business Stakeholder, Other. Manage groups merges defaults with saved list and groups in use.
- **People:** Name, username, role, group, email, phone, notes. Seed list provided for new installs (e.g. CDO team, Legal, HR, Business Stakeholders, IT/Security); you can add/edit/remove.

**Other data**
- **Engagements:** Optional list linked to workstreams; decisions, risks, metrics can be scoped to workstream or engagement.
- **Tasks / milestones:** Tasks can have workstream, owner, due date, status, priority; milestones migrated from legacy milestone list where applicable.
- **Inbox, meetings, decisions, risks, metrics, report notes:** Initialized empty or with minimal defaults; structure supports workstream/engagement linkage.

## Access

- **Team password:** Required when you open the ATLAS site (PageCrypt). Same for everyone; get it from your admin.
- **PIN:** Sign in with username and PIN after the page loads; required on first visit or after ~24 hours inactive (or new browser/device).
